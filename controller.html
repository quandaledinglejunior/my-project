Product Description

Precision Control Without Drift

Never worry about stick drift again! The Nova WGP14 V2 features premium anti-drift hall effect sticks, delivering ultra-precise circularity input without dead zones. Experience true control without hassle, thanks to authentic K-Silver JH16 hall-effect sticks that offer superior centering and deadzone performance compared to other hall-effect controllers.

Connectivity Without Limits

Seamlessly switch between wired and Bluetooth 5.3 connections, ensuring compatibility across a plethora of devices for uninterrupted gaming sessions. Enjoy the freedom of wireless connectivity without compromising on responsiveness or performance.

Precision in Every Move

Enjoy three-level adjustable Turbo mode and an anti-drift hall effect stick for unparalleled accuracy and control, giving you the edge in every game. Whether you're executing quick maneuvers or precise shots, the WGP14 V2 responds flawlessly to your commands.

Engaging Sensory Experience

Immerse yourself with an active motion sensor, rumble feedback, and a responsive touchpad, elevating your gaming experience to new heights of immersion and interaction. Feel every action and movement in-game for a truly captivating experience.

Audio Amplified

Experience rich soundscapes with the built-in audio port and speaker, enhancing the atmosphere of your games with crystal-clear audio wherever you play. Communicate seamlessly with teammates and enjoy immersive in-game sounds without additional accessories.

Rapid Recharge, Endless Adventure

Spend less time waiting and more time gaming with a quick two-hour charging time, ensuring you’re always ready to embark on gaming adventures across a multitude of compatible platforms. The long-lasting battery life keeps you in the game longer.
