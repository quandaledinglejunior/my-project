Introducing Xbox Series X

Introducing Xbox Series X, the fastest, most powerful Xbox ever. Play thousands of titles from four generations of consoles—all games look and play best on Xbox Series X.

Xbox Velocity Architecture

Experience next-gen speed and performance with the Xbox Velocity Architecture, powered by a custom SSD and integrated software. This architecture enables faster load times, seamless gameplay, and the ability to switch between multiple games in an instant.

Backward Compatibility

Play thousands of games from four generations of Xbox with backward compatibility, including optimized titles at launch. Enjoy your favorite classics alongside the latest releases with enhanced performance and visuals.

Xbox Game Pass Ultimate

Download and play over 100 high-quality games, including all new Xbox Game Studios titles like Halo Infinite the day they release, with Xbox Game Pass Ultimate (membership sold separately).

Xbox Smart Delivery

Xbox Smart Delivery ensures you play the best available version of your game no matter which console you’re playing on. Upgrade seamlessly to enhanced versions when available, ensuring optimal performance and visuals.
