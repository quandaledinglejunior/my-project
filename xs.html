Introducing the Xbox Series S

Introducing the Xbox Series S, the smallest, sleekest Xbox console ever. Experience the speed and performance of a next-gen all-digital console at an accessible price point. Go all-digital and enjoy disc-free, next-gen gaming with the smallest Xbox console ever made.

Xbox Velocity Architecture

Experience next-gen speed and performance with the Xbox Velocity Architecture, powered by a custom SSD and integrated software. This architecture enables faster load times, seamless gameplay, and the ability to switch between multiple games in an instant.

Access Your Favorite Entertainment

Access your favorite entertainment through apps like YouTube, Netflix, and more. Enjoy a versatile multimedia experience alongside your gaming sessions.

Xbox Game Pass Trial

Enjoy over 100 games right out of the box with a 1-month Xbox Game Pass trial. Discover new titles and enjoy a vast library of games across multiple genres.

4K Streaming and Blu-ray Movies

Watch 4K Blu-ray movies and stream 4K video on Netflix, Amazon, Hulu, Microsoft Movies & TV, and more. Experience stunning visuals and immersive sound quality.

Play with Friends and Family

Play with friends and family near and far—sitting together on the sofa or connecting with players around the world on Xbox Live, the fastest, most reliable gaming network.

Backward Compatibility

Xbox One games and accessories work together seamlessly, ensuring you can continue enjoying your favorite titles and peripherals without any hassle.
