Product Description

Immersive Display Experience

Experience unparalleled visual immersion with the MSI MAG 341CQP featuring a QD-OLED panel that delivers vibrant colors and deep blacks. The UWQHD resolution of 3440×1440 ensures sharp and detailed images, making it perfect for gaming, streaming, and professional work.

High Refresh Rate & Ultra Low Response Time

Enjoy smooth and fluid gameplay with a refresh rate of 170Hz and an ultra-fast response time of 0.03ms GTG. Whether you're engaged in fast-paced action games or watching high-motion videos, the MSI MAG 341CQP ensures minimal motion blur and ghosting.

Advanced HDR & Color Accuracy

With DisplayHDR True Black 400 and 1.07 Billion colors (10-bit), this monitor provides stunning contrast and color precision. The Adobe RGB, DCI-P3, and sRGB coverage ensures accurate color representation, making it ideal for content creators and gamers alike.

Dynamic Refresh Rate Technology

Equipped with AMD FreeSync™ Premium Pro, the MSI MAG 341CQP synchronizes the monitor's refresh rate with your GPU, eliminating screen tearing and reducing stuttering for a seamless gaming experience.

Ergonomic & Stylish Design

The frameless 1800R curvature and anti-reflective surface treatment not only enhance aesthetics but also provide a more natural viewing angle, reducing eye strain during extended use.

Versatile Connectivity

Connect effortlessly with multiple devices using DisplayPort, HDMI®, USB Type-C®, and USB ports. The integrated earphone out ensures you can enjoy immersive audio without additional accessories.

Adjustable & User-Friendly Features

Customize your viewing experience with adjustable tilt, swivel, and height settings. The Kensington Lock adds an extra layer of security, while the dynamic polling rate and activated range of 48~175Hz cater to various gaming needs.
