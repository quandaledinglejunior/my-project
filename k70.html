Product Description

K70 RGB PRO

MECHANICAL GAMING KEYBOARD

THE LEGEND CONTINUES

The K70 RGB PRO retains the iconic elements of our award-winning K70 series with a durable aluminum frame, CHERRY MX mechanical keyswitches, and per-key RGB backlighting, while setting a new bar for performance with AXON technology and a tournament switch.

POWERED BY AXON

AXON delivers a new level of performance by processing and transmitting your inputs up to 8x faster than conventional gaming keyboards with 8,000Hz hyper-polling, 4,000Hz key scanning, and up to 20 layers of hardware RGB lighting processing while maintaining speed performance.

CHERRY MX RGB SWITCHES

CHERRY MX RGB BROWN – TACTILE AND QUIET: 100% mechanical keyswitches featuring a tactile bump mid-press for gamers and typists looking for light feedback.
CHERRY MX RGB RED – LINEAR AND SMOOTH: 100% mechanical keyswitches featuring linear travel with a silky smooth keypress that’s ideal for performance gamers.
CHERRY MX SPEED RGB SILVER – LINEAR AND FAST: 100% mechanical keyswitches delivering lightning-fast response times with a 1.2mm actuation distance, perfect for fast-paced gameplay.
CHERRY MX RGB BLUE – TACTILE AND CLICKY: 100% mechanical keyswitches featuring a small bump when pressed with an audible click, resulting in a satisfying typing experience that you both hear and feel.
CHERRY MX SILENT RGB RED – LINEAR AND QUIET: 100% mechanical keyswitches featuring linear travel with a patented noise reduction design for a soft and quiet keypress, excellent for noise-conscious streamers and gamers.

DURABLE PBT DOUBLE-SHOT PRO KEYCAPS

A precision-molded keycap set with a standard bottom row layout resists wear, fading, and shine, with 1.5mm thickness for rigid stability.

K70 RGB PRO & iCUE

Powerful CORSAIR iCUE software on both Windows and macOS enables dynamic RGB lighting control, key remaps and macro programming, and exclusive iCUE integrations with select games that turn your entire system’s lighting into an extension of the action.

  • PER-KEY RGB LIGHTING
  • PROGRAMMABLE KEY ASSIGNMENTS
  • CUSTOM POLLING RATE
  • SAVE UP TO 50 PROFILES WITH 8MB STORAGE
