Product Description

Introducing the Nintendo Switch – OLED Model

Meet the newest member of the Nintendo Switch family. The new system features a vibrant 7-inch OLED screen, a wide adjustable stand, a dock with a wired LAN port, 64 GB of internal storage, and enhanced audio.

7-inch OLED Screen

Feast your eyes on vivid colors and crisp contrast when you play on-the-go. See the difference the vibrant screen makes, whether you’re racing at top speed or squaring off against enemies.

Wide, Adjustable Stand

Flip out the sturdy stand for easy viewing in Tabletop mode. Find your best angle by adjusting the stand to suit your comfort—perfect for a quick multiplayer game with a buddy.

Built-in Wired LAN Port

Connect online using the dock’s LAN port when playing in TV mode, ensuring a stable and fast internet connection for your gaming sessions.

64 GB Internal Storage

Save games to your system with 64 GB of internal storage, a portion of which is reserved for use by the system. Users can easily expand storage space using microSDHC or microSDXC cards up to 2TB (sold separately).

Enhanced Audio

Enjoy enhanced audio from the system’s onboard speakers in Tabletop and Handheld modes, delivering a richer and more immersive sound experience.

Three Modes in One

Nintendo Switch and Nintendo Switch – OLED Model systems are designed to fit your life, transforming from home console to portable system in a snap!

TV Mode

Dock your system to enjoy HD gaming on your TV.

Handheld Mode

Pick it up and play with the Joy-Con™ controllers attached to enjoy the 7-inch OLED screen.

Tabletop Mode

Use the wide, adjustable stand to position your console for easy viewing and gameplay.

Choose Your Control Style

One controller or two, vertical or sideways, attached to the console or separate. You can play multiple ways, depending on the game.

Share with a Friend

Pass a single Joy‑Con to a friend for instant two-player fun. Or invite friends to bring their own for a gaming party!

Try Something New

HD Rumble puts you in the game with physical feedback, while the IR Motion Camera opens up new experiences.

Better Together: Multiplayer Gaming Options

Local Co-op: Play on the big screen at home with friends.

Local Wireless: Link up to eight Nintendo Switch and Nintendo Switch Lite systems together to play multiplayer games anywhere.

Online Play: Team up or face off with others online in compatible games with a Nintendo Switch Online membership.

More System Features

Nintendo eShop: Find and buy digital versions of full games and DLC.

Nintendo Switch Online: Access your membership’s benefits right from the system’s HOME menu.

News: Get the latest game updates, announcements, and more with this up-to-date news feed.

Album: Easily take and save screenshots using the Capture Button. Then view, add text, and share to social networks from your album.

So Many Games to Play

The Nintendo Switch family is home to exclusive games from franchises like Super Smash Bros.™, The Legend of Zelda™, Mario Kart™, and many more. You’ll also find great games from other developers—with more added every week!

Nintendo Switch – OLED Model Specifications

General

Brand: Nintendo

Weight: 1.51 kg

Dimensions: 16.5 × 7.4 × 20.1 cm

Warranty: 1 Year

Color Options: Neon Red/Neon Blue, White, Mario Kart 8 Deluxe Edition, Mario Red Edition, The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom Edition

Physical Specifications

Size: 4 inches high, 9.5 inches long, and 0.55 inches deep (with Joy-Con attached)

Additional Depth: The depth from the tip of the analog sticks to the tip of the ZL/ZR buttons is 1.12 inches

Weight: Approximately 0.71 lbs (Approximately 0.93 lbs with Joy-Con controllers attached)

Display

Screen: Multi-touch capacitive touch screen / 7.0 inch OLED screen / 1280×720

Processor

CPU/GPU: NVIDIA Custom Tegra processor

Storage

Internal Storage: 64 GB

Users can easily expand storage space using microSDHC or microSDXC cards up to 2TB (sold separately).

Connectivity

Wireless: Wi-Fi (IEEE 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac compliant) / Bluetooth 4.1

Video Output: Up to 1080p via HDMI in TV mode / Up to 720p via built-in screen in Tabletop mode and Handheld modes

Audio Output: Compatible with 5.1ch Linear PCM output

Output via HDMI connector in TV mode

Audio

Speakers: Stereo

Audio Output: Compatible with 5.1ch Linear PCM output

Controls

Buttons: Power button / Volume button

USB Connector: USB Type-C (Used for charging or for connecting to the Nintendo Switch dock)

Headphone/Mic Jack: 3.5mm 4-pole stereo (CTIA standard)

Storage Slots

Game Card Slot: Nintendo Switch game cards

microSD Card Slot: Compatible with microSD, microSDHC, and microSDXC memory cards

*Once the microSDXC card is inserted, a system update will be necessary. An internet connection is required to perform this system update.

Sensors

Sensor: Accelerometer, gyroscope, and brightness sensor

Battery

Internal Battery: Lithium-ion battery / 4310mAh

Battery Life: Approximately 4.5 – 9 hours (The battery life will depend on the games you play. For instance, the battery will last approximately 5.5 hours for The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild.)

Charging Time: Approximately 3 hours (*When charging while the hardware is in sleep mode)

Operating Environment

Temperature Range: 41-95 degrees F

Humidity Range: 20-80% humidity
