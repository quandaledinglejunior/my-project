Product Description

Immersive Wireless Connectivity

Experience freedom with lightweight wireless connectivity using both 2.4 GHz and Bluetooth®, allowing seamless switching between devices without compromising on performance.

Superior Sound Quality

Equipped with exclusive 50 mm ASUS Essence drivers and an airtight chamber design, the Delta S delivers rich, immersive soundscapes that enhance every gaming session.

Advanced Microphone Technology

AI Beamforming Microphones with AI Noise Cancelation ensure exceptional voice pick-up, reducing over 500 million types of background noise for crystal-clear in-game communication.

Long-Lasting Battery Life

Fast charging provides up to 3 hours of use with just a 15-minute charge, and enjoy up to 25 hours of total battery life for uninterrupted gaming sessions.

Ergonomic Design

The lightweight design with ergonomic D-shaped ear cushions ensures long-lasting comfort, allowing you to game for hours without fatigue.

Extensive Compatibility

Compatible with PCs, Macs, PlayStation® 5, Nintendo Switch™, and mobile devices, the Delta S offers versatile connectivity options for all your gaming platforms.
